NoScam is privacy-first by design. All AI processing happens on your device. We never record your calls, never upload audio to the cloud, and never sell your data. Period.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Email Address: When you join our waitlist, we collect your email to notify you about product updates and launch.
  • Device Information: Basic device type (mobile/desktop) to understand our audience.

1.2 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Call recordings or audio data
  • Conversation transcripts
  • Your contacts or address book
  • Call logs or phone numbers you call/receive
  • Location data
  • Any personally identifiable information beyond your email

2. How NoScam Works (Privacy Architecture)

NoScam is designed with a privacy-first architecture:

  • On-Device Processing: All AI analysis happens locally on your phone. Audio never leaves your device.
  • No Cloud Upload: We do not transmit, store, or process your call audio on external servers.
  • Ephemeral Analysis: Audio is analyzed in real-time and immediately discarded. Nothing is saved.
  • No Third-Party Sharing: We never share, sell, or provide your data to advertisers or data brokers.

3. How We Use Your Information

The email address you provide is used solely to:

  • Notify you when the app launches
  • Provide early access and promotional offers to waitlist members

We will never sell your email to third parties or send you unrelated marketing.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your email address:

  • Encrypted data storage
  • Secure HTTPS connections
  • Limited access to personal data
