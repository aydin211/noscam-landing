NoScam is privacy-first by design. All AI processing happens on your device. We never record your calls, never read your messages, never upload data to the cloud, and never sell your data. Period.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Email Address: When you join our waitlist, we collect your email to notify you about product updates.
  • Device Information: Basic device type (mobile/desktop) to understand our audience.

1.2 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Call recordings or audio data
  • Message content from SMS, WhatsApp, or any app
  • Conversation transcripts
  • Your contacts or address book
  • Call logs or phone numbers
  • Location data

2. How NoScam Works (Privacy Architecture)

NoScam is designed with a privacy-first architecture:

  • On-Device Processing: All AI analysis happens locally on your phone. Audio and text never leave your device.
  • No Cloud Upload: We do not transmit, store, or process your communications on external servers.
  • Ephemeral Analysis: Content is analyzed in real-time and immediately discarded. Nothing is saved.
  • Accessibility API: For messaging app protection, we use Android's Accessibility Service to analyze on-screen text only when active. We never store this data.

  • Provide early access and promotional offers
